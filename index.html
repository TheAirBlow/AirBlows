Information

Flying Cards

The entire website is based on flying cards, which have realistic bouncing. To find what you're looking for, first locate the card, aim and click at it. On phones, speed of cards is capped at a much lower value, so it'd be easier.

Dragging

You can also forcibly drag flying cards. All other flying cards will still interact with it, but the one you are dragging will not be affected. This also means that you can drag it out of the window, which is very intentional. Being able to bounce other flying cards into the abyss is also, very, very intentional :D

Actions

There's 3 types of actions a flying card can perform:

  • Open a URL in a new tab
  • Go to the previous menu
  • Open a new menu

Buttons

You should've noticed 3 buttons below:

  • Reset - Randomizes position and velocity
  • Pause - Pauses physics and velocity
  • Help - Shows this window
